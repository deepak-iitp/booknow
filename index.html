Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about booking events, using our platform, or organizing your own? Check out the answers to some of the most common questions below. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us at any time!

Why BookNow why not Book...Show ?

We are committed to giving back to the community through our BookForGood initiative, making entertainment more accessible to underserved communities and lesser price then any one in market.

Do we need to create account for booking ticket ?

No, we do not encourage creating an account or sharing unnecessary data with us. However, providing basic information is necessary to verify your identity.

Where to find movies and events ?

You don't need to sturggel any more in life for booking a simple movie or event tickets on BookNow. In map screen you will find the nearest movie and event which is going on just click and book.

Can i cancel the tickets ?

Yes, we are the first in the market to prioritize saving your money in all aspects. You can cancel your ticket up to 2 hours before the event time and receive an instant refund, minus a minimal transaction charge from a third party.

How to organize my own event ?

You can easily click the "Add" button on the home screen and fill out the event form. You can choose to make the event private or public. Private events will only be visible to invited guests on their map, along with all relevant details and locations.

How to verify someone ticket ?

You can share the event creator account access with multiple team members. They simply need to scan the QR code of the ticket to verify all the necessary details. The person can also mark entry as done.
